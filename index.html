BUTTONS

Limbs includes two types of buttons: a normal one and an icon variety. Buttons are 38px high and icon buttons are 38x38px. It is up to you to decide what to put in them. Also, margin and padding are up to you to implement based on your icon size. This page uses the feather icon pack.
